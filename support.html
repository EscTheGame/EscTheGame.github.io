Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs by emailing us at esc.apethegamee@gmail.com.

What platforms is the game available on?

Esc. | The Game is available on PC, Mac, and mobile devices.

How do I save my progress?

The game automatically saves your progress. Make sure to be connected to the internet to ensure your progress is saved to the cloud.

Can I play with friends?

Yes, Esc. | The Game supports multiplayer mode. You can invite your friends to play together.

Are there any in-game purchases?

Yes, the game offers in-game purchases for cosmetic items and additional content. However, these purchases are optional and do not affect gameplay.

If you need to reset your account, please contact our support team at esc.apethegamee@gmail.com.

What are the system requirements for the game?

The minimum system requirements are:

  • OS: Windows 10 / macOS 10.13
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or equivalent
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Is there a mobile version of the game?

Yes, Esc. | The Game has a mobile version available for both Android and iOS devices.

We aim to release updates regularly, typically every month. These updates include bug fixes, new features, and improvements.

Is there a single-player mode?

Yes, Esc. | The Game offers a rich single-player experience in addition to its multiplayer mode.
